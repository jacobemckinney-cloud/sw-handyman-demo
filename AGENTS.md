# Project Instructions

This is a simple one-page website demo for S.W. Handyman.

## Goals
- Keep the site professional, simple, and mobile-friendly.
- Use plain HTML, CSS, and minimal JavaScript.
- Do not add React, frameworks, build tools, or extra dependencies unless asked.
- Keep the design close to the S.W. Handyman van branding: dark charcoal, white, clean all-caps style, and warm wood accents.
- Use real business details only when provided or verified.
- Do not invent reviews, addresses, business hours, licenses, guarantees, or extra services.

## Files
- Main website file: `index.html`
- Images should go in the `images/` folder.
- Image filenames should stay consistent with the code.

## Image filenames
- `sw-handyman-van.jpeg`
- `sw-handyman-storefront.jpeg`
- `sw-handyman-wood-ceiling.jpeg`
- `sw-handyman-kitchen.jpeg`
- `sw-handyman-interior-floor.jpeg`

## Review checklist
Before finishing changes, check:
- The site works on mobile.
- Phone and email links work.
- Images load correctly.
- No fake testimonials are presented as real.
- The copy sounds professional but local and simple.
